Introduction

   

Tristan Schrager is a self-taught artist based on the Island of Oahu. He focuses on ink illustration, mixed media painting, and graphic design.

   

Inspiration

   

Spending most of his free time in the water, Tristan's inspiration comes from finding beauty in the uncontrollable quality of nature. Tristan continues to develop his technique and style by drawing from nature, culture, the people around him and the places he visits.
